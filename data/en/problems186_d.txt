Problem Statement
Given are N integers A_1,ldots,A_N.
Find the sum of |A_i-A_j| over all pairs i,j such that 1≦ i < j ≦ N.
In other words, find displaystyle{sum_{i=1}^{N-1}sum_{j=i+1}^{N} |A_i-A_j|}.

Constraints
2 ≦ N ≦ 2 times 10^5
|A_i|≦ 10^8
A_i is an integer.

InputInput is given from Standard Input in the following format:
N
A_1 ldots A_N

Output
Print the answer.

Sample Input 1
3
5 1 2

Sample Output 1
8
We have |5-1|+|5-2|+|1-2|=8.

Sample Input 2
5
31 41 59 26 53

Sample Output 2
176
