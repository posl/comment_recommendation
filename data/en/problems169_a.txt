Problem Statement
Compute A times B.

Constraints
1 ≦ A ≦ 100
1 ≦ B ≦ 100
All values in input are integers.

InputInput is given from Standard Input in the following format:
A B

Output
Print the value A times B as an integer.

Sample Input 1
2 5

Sample Output 1
10
We have 2 times 5 = 10.

Sample Input 2
100 100

Sample Output 2
10000
